2003 - 2016

Mirac Abay was born on 13.04.2003 in Erzurum. From an early age, he developed various hobbies and achieved many successes, such as winning the Erzurum Chess Provincial Championship, Erzurum Karate Provincial Championship, and Eastern Anatolia Karate Regional Third Place. Later, he passed the high school entrance exam and was accepted into Mecidiye Anatolian High School.

2016 - 2021

During his high school education, he developed his skills in computers and software and added new hobbies to his life. After high school, he took the exam for his desired field, Computer Engineering, and was accepted into the English Computer Engineering Department at Atatürk University.

2021 - 2025

In the first years of university, he began an internship in geometry at Alp Epsilon Educational Institutions. He became an active member of several clubs, including the Computer and Informatics Club, and rose to the position of a manager. After two years, he left the clubs he was involved in and also concluded his internship in geometry, focusing entirely on his courses and personal development for almost a year.

Present

He is currently a fourth-year student and continues his geometry internship at Optimum Educational Institutions and his Full-Stack Developer internship at Missoft Software Company.
